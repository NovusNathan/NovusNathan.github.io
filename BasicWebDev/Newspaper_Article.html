Qdoba Opens in Hartford

Over the weekend Hartford experienced the addition of a new restaurant. Qdoba is now a part of Hartford life and brings their unique menu selections, quality food and good service. Menu features items with steak, beef, pork and chicken. Qdoba offers a variety of beans, rices, sauces and other vegetable toppings.

Convenient Location

Taking the place of the old burnt down Culver's, Qdoba is right of the most busy street in Harftford, Sumner Avenue.

Adequate Parking

Qdoba has its own lot and on busy days there are two other large parking lots located adjacent to Qdoba's own lot easily within walking distance.

Custom Specialty Service

Should you be in a hurry Qdoba also has a drivethru and also accepts phone in orders. Handicap access is present.
